# Notes

## Core idea
Decision theory can help design better AI systems, better workflows, and better content strategies.

## Key themes
- strategic reasoning
- uncertainty
- risk and reward
- human + AI decision loops
- long-term vs short-term optimization
- decision quality vs output quality

## Early observations
- Many AI workflows optimize for speed, not decision quality.
- Good systems are not only fast, they are robust.
- Human judgment still matters in high-stakes decisions.
- AI can assist reasoning, but should not replace strategic thinking.
- Most teams need better decision architecture, not just better tools.
